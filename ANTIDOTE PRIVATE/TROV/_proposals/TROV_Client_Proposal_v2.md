## The Opportunity

TROV is a collectibles event discovery platform with a working prototype already validated against real data and user testing. The app surfaces events across card shows, autograph signings, athlete appearances, mail-in signings, memorabilia events, collector meetups, giveaways, and brand drops — with smart personalization based on location, sports leagues, followed athletes and teams, and individual collector preferences.

The v1 prototype demonstrates a production-ready product concept with four functioning routes, a complete preference engine, event detail pages with athlete rosters, and real data integration. Our engagement turns that proven foundation into a native iOS app on the App Store — built to Apple's standards with the polish collectors expect.

### Why July 15?

Fanatics Fest runs July 16–19 at the Javits Center in New York City — the largest NBA signing event ever assembled. With confirmed appearances from Allen Iverson, Scottie Pippen, Karl-Anthony Towns, Trae Young, Jalen Brunson, Dennis Rodman, and nine other NBA legends, this event draws the exact audience TROV is built for. Being live on the App Store the day Fanatics Fest opens is the highest-leverage launch moment TROV will have in 2026.
