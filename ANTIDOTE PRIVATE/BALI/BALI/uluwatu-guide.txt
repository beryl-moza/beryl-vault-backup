BERYL'S ULUWATU GUIDE
A personal list of favorites on the Bukit — not sponsored, just places I genuinely love.
————————————————————————————

☕ COFFEE
• The Dharma Experience — My personal favorite. So big and comfortable with tons of spots to settle in. Great coffee and food. Start your day here and just stay to work.
• %Arabica (at Alchemy) — Super special Japanese specialty coffee. Lots of friends meet here mornings for a strong coffee before moving into Alchemy to work for hours.
• Analog — Very cool. Meticulously sourced beans, turmeric lattes, minimalist design on Labuansait. Plan at least one morning here for coffee and breakfast.
• Drifter Surf Shop & Cafe — A must. Super vibe, beautiful garden seating, yummy chocolates and food. Great coffee in a proper surfer setting.
• BGS Bali — Very very good coffee near Suluban Beach. Strong wifi, power outlets everywhere.

————————————————————————————

💻 COWORK / WORK SPOTS
• The Dharma Experience — Spacious and comfortable, so many spots to find your corner. My go-to.
• Alchemy — Settle in for hours. Plant-based cafe with wifi, great juices, yoga, incredible chocolates, and the best coconut soft serve on the Bukit.
• BGS Bali — Great wifi, power outlets, easy to work here all morning.

————————————————————————————

🍛 WARUNGS
• Warung Local (Bingin Beach) — MY FAVORITE. Build-your-plate nasi campur buffet. Fresh, authentic, incredibly cheap. The real deal.
• Yeye's Warung — 25+ years and still great. Classic nasi campur, laid-back. A proper Uluwatu institution.

————————————————————————————

🍣 SUSHI
• Sushimi — Conveyor belt sushi on Labuansait. Fun concept, great quality, open 11am-11pm. Wednesdays ALL plates drop to 20k IDR — go on a Wednesday.
• Yuki — Higher-end Japanese. Wood-fired and charcoal sushi, wagyu yakitori, tuna truffle maki. Moody two-storey space. Book ahead for weekends.

————————————————————————————

🥩 STEAK
• Papi Sapi — Pick your cut from the display chiller and they grill it to perfection. Casual neighborhood grill. Open 4pm-11:30pm.
• Tusk Grill & Bar — Amazing. Fire-grilled premium Australian steaks in Bingin.
• Mason — Wood-fired grill, incredible 400g ribeye. Stylish, moody space on Labuansait. Open noon-midnight.

————————————————————————————

🍷 DINNER
• Gooseberry — Modern French above Bingin Beach. Tuna tataki, ravioli, soufflé pancakes. Hidden alley gem. Book ahead.
• KALA — New Greek-Mediterranean (opened 2024) near Padang Padang. Wood-fired seafood and meats, great natural wines and speakeasy cocktails.
• The Cave by Chef Ryan Clift — Limestone cave 70m above the ocean. 7-10 course tasting menus, candlelit, only 22 seats. Book well ahead — special occasion.
• Rockfish — Fine dining in a cave on the cliffs. Glass-floored terrace, ex-NOBU chef. Seafood-focused.
• El Kabron — Spanish Mediterranean on the cliff. Known for the best sunset in Bali. Book ahead for sunset.
• Bartolo — French-Italian bistro. Beef bourguignon, grilled prawns, handcrafted cocktails.
• L'Osteria — Authentic Neapolitan pizza — sourdough risen, properly blistered. Great handmade pastas too.
• BB52 — Best craft burgers in Uluwatu. Great post-surf. Late-night option.

————————————————————————————

🧃 JUICE & SMOOTHIES
• Ubud Kitchen Juice Bar — Like going back in time. The sweetest ladies who give samples. Stock up on 3-4 bottles at a fraction of normal juice bar prices. Big jars of coconut water. Open til 11pm!
• Alchemy — Cold-pressed juices, superfood smoothies, healing tonics. All plant-based.

————————————————————————————

🌅 SUNSET SPOTS
• Bingin Cliff — My go-to spot to bring people for sunset. Just the cliff above Bingin. Free, no crowds, pure magic.
• Single Fin — THE classic Uluwatu sunset. Cliff terraces overlooking the surf. Legendary Sundays.
• Mana — Infinity pool, open-air pavilion, uninterrupted ocean views. Friday sunset happy hour 4-6pm.
• Karang Boma Cliff — Epic panoramic ocean views. Only 10k IDR. 5 min from Nyang Nyang.
• Uluwatu Temple (Pura Luhur) — Sacred temple on 70m cliffs. Kecak Fire Dance at sunset. 30k IDR.

————————————————————————————

🏄 BEACHES
• Bingin Beach — Boho vibes, reef breaks, cliffside warungs. The community hub. Free.
• Padang Padang — Famous Eat Pray Love cove. Intimate, turquoise, stunning cliffs.
• Balangan Beach — Golden sand, great sunsets, afternoon surf. Chilled out.
• Thomas Beach — Super fun for learning to surf. Beginner-friendly, laid-back local vibe.
• Nyang Nyang Beach — Remote, untouched, wild. Steep 1.5km walk but worth it.
• Green Bowl Beach — Secluded cove, 330 jungle stairs, natural cave. 12k IDR.

————————————————————————————

🎶 MUSIC & WEEKLY EVENTS
• Monday — Mana: Friday Sunset (happy hour 4-6pm, DJs, pool)
• Tuesday — Tabu: Sushi & Afro-Latin beats (Moyo Tuesdays)
• Wednesday — Tabu: Afrohouse DJ night | Sushimi: All sushi plates 20k IDR
• Thursday — Tabu: Sushi & Beats | Cashew Tree: Thirsty Thursday (live music til midnight)
• Friday — Mana: Sunset | Lola's: Reggaeton night
• Sunday — Single Fin: Sunday Sessions (legendary, ~150k IDR cover, arrive early) | Atmos: Sip & Steam Sundays (4-10pm)

————————————————————————————

💪 GYMS & FITNESS
• Bambu Fitness (Bingin) — World-class gym. CrossFit, HIIT, pilates, cycling. Oasis Recovery Centre with sauna, ice baths, jacuzzi & pool. Drop-in 225k IDR.
• 360 Move — HIIT, hot pilates, Muay Thai, dance, spin.

————————————————————————————

🧘 YOGA
• Alchemy Yoga & Meditation — Multiple styles, drop-in 160k IDR. Ice bath + sauna on site.
• La Tribu — Vinyasa, Power, Yin, Surf yoga. Drop-in 150k IDR. Daily classes.
• Morning Light Yoga — Open-air teak shala with jungle + wave views. 150k IDR includes coconut water. No booking needed.

————————————————————————————

♨️ SAUNA & STEAM
• Santai Recovery Spa (Bingin) — Stadium-style sauna with panoramic glass, group ice bath (fits 8-10 people), hot pool. Peaceful and spacious.
• Atmos Social — Guided steam rituals. Sip & Steam Sundays 4-10pm with live music and cold plunge. Special experience.
• Bambu Fitness Oasis — Sauna, ice baths, jacuzzi, pool.

————————————————————————————

💆 MASSAGE
• Spring Spa (next to Alchemy) — Ask for Seven. Not your average Bali massage — he is a serious healer, next level. Come prepared for a real healing experience. Hard to get an appointment so book early.

————————————————————————————

🛒 GROCERIES
• Nirmala Supermarket — Big local supermarket, Jl. Uluwatu Ungasan. Wide variety, liquor, French bakery. Budget-friendly. 7am-10pm.
• Papa's Deli / Mood by Ours — Organic market on Labuansait. Farm-fresh veg, gourmet groceries, no-seed-oil products.
• Ubud Kitchen Juice Bar — Also great for stocking your villa fridge with fresh juices and coconut water.
