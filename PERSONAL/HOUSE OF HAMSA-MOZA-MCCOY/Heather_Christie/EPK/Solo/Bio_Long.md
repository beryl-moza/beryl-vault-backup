## Long bio (250 words)
Heather Christie is a DJ, producer, vocalist, mama, and mentor based in California whose artistry lives where the dancefloor meets ceremony. She crafts immersive sonic journeys that blend electronic beats with her own live vocals, moving fluidly between organic house, Afro house, Latin house, and medicine music. Her sets are built for long arcs — sunrise to midnight, beach club to ecstatic dance floor — always holding space for both body and spirit.

With 113,566 monthly Spotify listeners and over 8 million total streams, Heather's catalog includes the 2025 album CELESTIAL, 2024's EROS, and 2023's For my brother / For my daughter. Her top tracks — "All in Time" (2.7M plays), "After the Fire" (2.5M plays, with Vir McCoy), and the EINKI remix of "Broken Whole" (808K plays) — have earned her placement alongside KR3TURE, Scott Nice, SaQi, and Poranguí in the organic/medicine house ecosystem.

Heather is the founder of Moonbabe Records and Mind Body Music, a holistic online school that mentors women across the full arc of musical creation — composition, production, performance — through programs like The Sound Dojo and Source to Stream. She describes her work as that of a "musical midwife and sonic doula," helping artists birth the sound already alive inside them.

She has been featured in Ladygunn Magazine and counts Erykah Badu among those who have publicly endorsed her work. Contact: hello@mindbodymusic.co.
