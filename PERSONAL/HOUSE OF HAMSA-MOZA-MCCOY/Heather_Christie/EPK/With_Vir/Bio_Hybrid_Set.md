# Heather Christie + Vir McCoy — Hybrid Set

## What it is
Heather Christie's DJ set with Vir McCoy playing live alongside her on percussion, flutes, and guimbri (Gnawa bass). Not a duo act — this is her set, elevated by live instrumentation that lands especially well in outdoor venues, ceremonial contexts, and extended sunset slots.

## One-liner
Husband-and-wife hybrid set: Heather Christie (DJ / live vocals) + Vir McCoy (live percussion, flutes, guimbri) weaving electronic and ceremonial over a DJ spine.

## Short bio (60 words)
Heather Christie (113K monthly Spotify listeners, 8M+ streams) brings her organic/Afro/medicine house DJ sets to life with husband and collaborator Vir McCoy (109K monthly listeners; has performed with Mickey Hart's band and licensed music with Spike Lee) on live percussion, flutes, and guimbri. The result: a DJ set with live ceremonial texture — built for sunsets, beach clubs, and ecstatic dance floors.
